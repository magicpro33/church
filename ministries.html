Serving God and Our Community

Our Ministries

Worship & Prayer

Sunday School at 10:00 AM and Worship Service at 11:00 AM. All are welcome to join us in praising God together.

Community Outreach

Our Blessing Box provides food and daily essentials to neighbors in need. We actively support local families and those facing hardship.

Grief Support Group

A caring, faith-based space for those who have lost loved ones. Weekly meetings offer comfort, prayer, and Christian fellowship.

Youth & Family Ministry

Helping children and families grow in faith through worship, activities, education, and community events.
