Nazareth Lutheran Church

A Welcoming ELCA Congregation in Lexington, SC

About Us

Nazareth Lutheran Church is a community of faith rooted in the Lutheran tradition and grounded in the love of Jesus Christ.

We gather every Sunday to worship, pray, learn, and support one another. Our mission is to gather in fellowship to pray, teach, and witness God's love in our community.

Whether you are new to church, returning after many years, or have been part of a Lutheran congregation your whole life, you are welcome here.

We are a congregation of the Evangelical Lutheran Church in America (ELCA).
